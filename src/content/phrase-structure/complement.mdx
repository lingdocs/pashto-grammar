## What's a complement?

A **complement** is a separate block in a <VP /> or <EP /> that describes an <NP /> (subject or object). 

For example, in the phrase:

**Note**: we said it's a "seperate block" but with <Link to="/compound-verbs/helper-verbs/">stative helper verbs</Link> the complement also gets <Link to="/compound-verbs/helper-verbs/#welding--splitting-the-complement--️">welded together with the verb</Link> in certain cases.
